Our Vision:
Successful Business

Velocity Xcelerator Pvt. Ltd is one of the leading Business Consulting and Corporate Training companies in Pune. Velocity is all about Speed towards Business growth. We provide simple tools, proven frameworks that will help you develop your next level leaders and AutoPilot your business.

Velocity Xcelerator offers various customized advisory programs to enhance employee effectiveness in different departments and operations as per the client's needs. We work closely with the client and enter into a long-term consulting assignment.

As a BestManagement consultant and Business coach, we take all services that fall under Business Coaching, Business Advisory, Strategy Consulting, HR Consulting, Sales & Distribution Consultancy, Operations Consulting, Business Planning & Goal Setting.

We helped and transformed more than 100 companies and 3500+ individuals leading to sustainable organizational Productivity improvement.

We help companies find the core purpose of their organization & Establish a vision and mission of their company to form an ethical foundation for sustained business results. We help businesses to understand their current business circumstances and operational challenges & facilitate brainstorming on their most pressing business challenges, work on strategy designing, defining specific organizational goals, enabling focused initiatives.

We started this journey with a vision to support aspiring entrepreneurs, SMEs, and Mid-sized Corporates sowing the seeds of designing their ideal business plan, Setting goals, People management, and building next-level leaders.

Velocity is one of the best Management Consulting & Corporate Training companies in Pune, implementing processes in organizations and evaluate the effectiveness through regular reviews and Training.
